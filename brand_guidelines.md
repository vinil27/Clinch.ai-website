# Brand Design Kit & Guidelines

## 1. Brand Foundations

### 1.1 Logo System
*To be replaced with final assets.*
- **Primary Logo**: [Icon + Wordmark] - Used for headers and primary branding.
- **Secondary Mark**: [Icon Only] - Used for favicons, avatars, and tight spaces.
- **Clear Space**: Maintain 24px (1.5rem) clear space around the logo at all times.

### 1.2 Color Palette
We use an earthy, professional palette inspired by premium design aesthetics.

**Primary Colors**
- **Deep Espresso (Primary Dark)**: `#3E2723` - Used for primary text, headings, and strong backgrounds.
- **Warm Cream (Primary Light)**: `#FDFBF7` - The main background color, creating a soft, paper-like feel.

**Accent Colors**
- **Rust Orange**: `#D35400` - Used for "Call to Action" buttons and highlights.
- **Sage Green**: `#556B2F` - Used for success states and nature-related accents.
- **Soft Clay**: `#E6DDC4` - Used for secondary backgrounds and cards.

**Functional Colors**
- **Error**: `#C0392B`
- **Success**: `#27AE60`
- **Warning**: `#F39C12`

### 1.3 Typography
**Headings: Serif (e.g., Merriweather, Playfair Display)**
- Gives a trustworthy, editorial, and premium feel.
- **H1**: 48px / 1.2 - Main Landing Prompts
- **H2**: 36px / 1.3 - Section Headers
- **H3**: 24px / 1.4 - Card Titles

**Body: Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, system-ui)**
- Ensures high readability for UI elements and long text.
- **Body Large**: 18px / 1.6
- **Body Regular**: 16px / 1.6
- **Caption**: 14px / 1.5

### 1.4 Iconography
- **Style**: Line icons with rounded corners.
- **Stroke Width**: 2px consistently.
- **Color**: Inherits current text color (usually Deep Espresso).
- **Libraries**: Lucide, Heroicons, or Phosphor.

### 1.5 Motion & Animation
- **Principles**: Subtle, smooth, and purposeful. No jarring flashes.
- **Durations**:
  - `Fast`: 200ms (Hover effects)
  - `Normal`: 300ms (Transitions, Modals)
  - `Slow`: 500ms (Entry animations)
- **Easing**: `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` (Standard ease-out).

## 2. Core UI Components

### 2.1 Buttons
- **Primary**: Solid Deep Espresso background, White text. Rounded pill shape.
- **Secondary**: Transparent background, Deep Espresso border.
- **Ghost**: Text only, Rust Orange on hover.

### 2.2 Cards
- **Background**: White or Soft Clay.
- **Border Radius**: 16px to 24px (highly rounded).
- **Shadow**: Soft, diffused shadow (`0 4px 20px rgba(62, 39, 35, 0.08)`).

### 2.3 Forms
- **Input Fields**:
  - Background: White.
  - Border: 1px solid `#E0E0E0`.
  - Focus: 2px solid Deep Espresso.
  - Radius: 12px.
